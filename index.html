What's so great about corgis?

Glad you asked...

The (s)ASS

Corgis have a level of self-esteem that is, frankly, hilarious. This inflated ego can often be seen from behind as they proudly strut their stuff walking ahead of you.

The Duality

With their thick coats, broad chests, and stunning patterns, corgis have the ability of looking every bit the Queen's favored pet. On the other hand, they also have moments like this...

The Smile

Corgis are intelligent, energetic, strong-willed, vocal dogs. If they have a problem with something, they won't hesitate to let you know. While their big personalities may come with some challenges, they have a magic cure to make it all better: their smile.
